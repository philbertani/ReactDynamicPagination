Most pagination schemes have the page navigation at the bottom and do not have very good contrast. The change in page number is usually too subtle to be noticed.  I am not sure why web developers go out of their way to make things almost unnoticable these days.  It especially does not work for older people or people who cannot see well.   

This pagination bar is at the top center and the number of items per page and pagination bar adjusts to the window size as well. This way a user can click through different pages and not have to constantly scroll up and down.
